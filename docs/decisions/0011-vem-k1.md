# ADR-0011: VEM k=1 for polyhedra

- Status: accepted (2026-07-10)

## Decision
Ship Virtual Element Method k=1 for general convex polyhedra as
`ElementType::kPolyVem` with face connectivity on `NodalElement`.

## Formulation
- Consistency: constant-strain B-bar via face integrals of hat functions.
- Stabilization: τ μ h (I−Π)ᵀ(I−Π) with Π the L2 projector onto the
  12-dimensional linear-displacement space (6 RBM + 6 constant strain).
- Patch test exact on hex-as-poly meshes (verified).

## Alternatives
- Wachspress/mean-value shape functions: more complex on non-convex cells.
- Isoparametric only: cannot represent general polyhedra.

## Why
Co-design path for hybrid meshes: poly transition cells share assembly with FEM.
